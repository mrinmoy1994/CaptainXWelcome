Work Culture

  • Home away from home: We, the founders of Captain X do not believe in serious office environment. What we believe is, to work smart one needs to be given the right atmosphere to work. With the help of our superb design team we created a spectacular homely atmosphere to work. Where any one can utilize their full potential as a part of the team.
  • Value for Team work: “Talent wins games, but teamwork and intelligence wins championship.” – Michael Jordon. Individual talent is important. But what is more important to us is work as a team towards the same goal. In Captain X everything we achieve, we do it as a team.
  • Creative mind is the winner of all: It takes lot to create something. A creative mind is always be the pillar of success. And in Captain X we cherish our team mates to use their creativity and deliver our attractive website and application for your utilization.
  • We do not believe in HARDWORK, we believe in SMARTWORK: Smart-work is nothing but Hard-work using knowledge and intelligence. One can achieve anything by working hard, but by working smart the person can achieve the same output in a lot lesser time. In Captain X we always encourage people to use smart-working.
  • Flexibility is always been the key to success: A flexible mind with a flexible attitude can adopt anything, where as a rigidity in working place can turn the most intelligent & resourceful person to fall. To ensure and enhance the creativity of our team mates we always make sure that all our colleagues always become flexible to work under any atmosphere.
  • We want to make a difference in Society: From our childhood we are being taught that Humans are social beings, and all of us have some social responsibilities. And now, when we all become grown-up person, we make sure that those lessons won’t go wasted. In Captain X we have a separate team who are looking after the Social Responsibilities and organise various social events. We work with different groups as well as individually to make a better society. Our works will be posted in our web blog from time to time.
  • Women Empowerment: India is a matriarch country. From ancient period to recent era, women had worked shoulder to shoulder with men. In Captain X, we work towards the empowerment and development of the women of our country.
  • Freedom of work: In Captain X we provide our employees to work independently. To enhance the quality of the work and increase the creative capacity of our employees we make sure that everyone can work without restrictions but with responsibility. We also admire and welcome new concepts, new thoughts.
