Captain X is an online fantasy gaming company which provides a platform to play fantasy cricket in cricket across the country.

Absolutely. It is legal as this is not a game of chance. This is a game of skills.

55. How the players’ credit will be determined?

Players’ credit will be determined on basis of players’ recent form, the value of the player in real team, importance of the series. Players’ credit will be same throughout the series. During any series, players’ credit will not be changed. Captain X has a dedicated research team who are constantly monitoring the players, the matches, the credits to decide the players’ credit.

Captain X has soul and absolute authority to determine the credit of any player. Under no circumstances, any captain can have any opinion about the determination of the players’ credit.
